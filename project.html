About VolTracker

Purpose

The purpose of this web application is to track information on volunteer sessions done by the members of Science National Honor Society (SNHS) at Clements High School. A member will use this tracker to:

  • Review the information about the last session at their location, including suggestions for their volunteering time
  • Log information about their volunteering session for future volunteers
